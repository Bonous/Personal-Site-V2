About Me

I'm Simon Gorbet, a 17-year-old first-year student at the University of Waterloo. I am a bachelor of applied science candidate studying mechatronics engineering. I am super interested in robots, specifically the design and implementation of robotics systems and mechanisms. I love working in teams with friends and like-minded peers to create awesome projects. Whether it be organizing eSports tournaments for local high school students or creating an entirely 3d printed quadrupedal leg, I will always be working on something I enjoy.
