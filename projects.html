Projects

Welcome to my projects page! Here you will find a collection of my some of my favourite projects. If you have more questions about any projects feel free to contact me.

Leggo - Prototype Quadrupedal Leg

A fully 3d printed 2 D.O.F. quadrupedal leg that I designed and built. Leggo combines several different skills including 3d printing, CAD, C++ firmware programming, inverse kinematics, circuit design, and rapid prototyping.

WARG Gimbal

A 2 axis motorized gimbal designed for medium-sized hobby planes. The gimbal is modeled entirely in SolidWorks for a Waterloo Aerial Robotics Group bootcamp.
