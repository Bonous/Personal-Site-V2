DoorMe - NewHacks



For the 24-hour NewHacks hackathon (hosted by UofT) my classmate and good friend Ryan Chan created DoorMe. It was a super fun project for us where we used Python, OpenCV, QR Code libraries, Arduino, 3D Printing, and SolidWorks. Our project ended up winning 1st place which was pretty awesome for our first hackathon. Feel free to check out the video above and the DevPost page for more details.
